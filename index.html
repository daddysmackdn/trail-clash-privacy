Trail Clash Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Trail Clash is published by Computer Office Solutions. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the Trail Clash mobile app.

Information We Collect

Trail Clash stores gameplay progress on your device, such as coins, chests, settings, unlocked skins, selected themes, best scores, and daily mission progress. This local gameplay data is not sent to us by the app.

Trail Clash uses Google Mobile Ads SDK and Google User Messaging Platform to show ads and manage consent where required. These services may collect or share approximate location inferred from IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and device or other identifiers for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Trail Clash uses Unity In-App Purchasing and Google Play Billing for optional purchases. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. We do not receive your credit card number or bank account details.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to show and measure ads, prevent fraud and abuse, diagnose app or SDK performance, process purchases, provide rewards, and keep the app functioning properly.

Third-Party Services

Trail Clash may use services provided by Google AdMob, Google Play Billing, and Unity In-App Purchasing. These providers process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Children

Trail Clash is not directed to children under 13. The app is intended for teen and adult audiences. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review the issue.

Data Choices

You can reset or delete your Android advertising ID in Android settings. You can also clear local Trail Clash progress by deleting the app or clearing the app's local storage on your device. Because Trail Clash does not create user accounts, there is no account profile to delete inside the app.

Security

Data transmitted by the third-party SDKs used in Trail Clash is encrypted in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we use standard platform services for ads and purchases.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or Trail Clash privacy practices, contact us at info@computerofficesolutions.com.
