Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

At a glance

Word Duel: Secret Rivals is published by Computer Office Solutions. The game is intended for players age 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. This policy explains how information is handled when you play on Android or iOS.

Information handled by the game

  • Platform identity. When you choose online play, Google Play Games or Apple Game Center may provide your verified display name and authentication credential. Unity Authentication uses that credential to create a pseudonymous player ID. Android and iOS players remain in separate platform pools.
  • Gameplay and room information. Match choices, secret words, guesses, scores, room codes, ranking data, and multiplayer status may be processed to operate matches, rooms, leaderboards, and anti-abuse features when those online services are available.
  • Local game data. Settings, progress, coins, boosts, cosmetic choices, and similar gameplay state may be stored on your device.
  • Advertising and consent data. Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform may process IP-derived approximate location, app interactions, advertising identifiers, device information, diagnostics, consent choices, and fraud-prevention signals.
  • Purchases. If optional purchases are offered, Google Play or Apple processes payment and purchase history. We do not receive your full payment-card or bank-account details.
  • Support communications. If you contact us, we receive the information in your message and your email address so we can respond.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate players, provide matches and rooms, calculate game results, operate leaderboards, remember game state, show and measure ads, honor privacy choices, process purchases and rewards, prevent fraud or abuse, troubleshoot technical problems, and respond to support requests.

Advertising and privacy choices

Advertising may be personalized only where applicable law and your consent permit it. You can review or change available advertising privacy choices from the game's Settings screen. You can also limit or reset advertising identifiers through Android or iOS settings. Computer Office Solutions does not directly sell personal information. Advertising partners may process or share identifiers and activity for advertising purposes as described in their own policies and subject to your available choices.

Third-party services

The game may rely on the following providers, which process data under their own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy, including Google Play Games, Google Play Billing, AdMob, and User Messaging Platform.
  • Unity Privacy Policy, including Unity Authentication and other enabled Unity Gaming Services.
  • Apple Privacy Policy, including Game Center and App Store purchases.

Data retention and deletion

Local data remains on your device until it is cleared or the app is removed. Platform providers and enabled online services retain information according to their own policies and operational needs. To request deletion of information controlled by Computer Office Solutions, email us with the platform you use and enough information to locate the relevant player record. You may also manage or delete platform information through your Google, Apple, or Unity-supported account controls where available.

Children's privacy

Word Duel: Secret Rivals is intended for players age 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the game, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

Security and international processing

We use platform services and reasonable safeguards intended to protect information. No transmission or storage method is completely secure. Third-party providers may process information in countries other than your own, subject to their published safeguards and applicable law.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Contact us to make a request. We may need to verify the request before acting on it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game or legal requirements change. The current version will remain posted at this address with an updated date.
